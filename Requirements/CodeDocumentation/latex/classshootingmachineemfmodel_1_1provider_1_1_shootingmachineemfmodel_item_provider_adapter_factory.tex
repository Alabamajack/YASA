\subsection{Detailed Description}
This is the factory that is used to provide the interfaces needed to support Viewers. The adapters generated by this factory convert E\-M\-F adapter notifications into calls to \hyperlink{classshootingmachineemfmodel_1_1provider_1_1_shootingmachineemfmodel_item_provider_adapter_factory_a804ba049fd79f648d54aad9033c9eecd}{fire\-Notify\-Changed}. The adapters also support Eclipse property sheets. Note that most of the adapters are shared among multiple instances.
